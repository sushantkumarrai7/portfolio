ABOUT PROJECT


Challenge:

Redesigning the Honeywell Unisim Tutor's UX according to the demands and standards of the current industry/users needs and helping the team move the product from legacy platforms to the new Honeywell Forge platform that provides new enhanced capabilities to the product and users.

About Unisim Tutor: Honeywell’s UniSim® Tutor is a game style tool that has wide application across process and manufacturing industries. By capturing, propagating and assessing, cause and effect knowledge and troubleshooting skills, UniSim Tutor helps develop and deliver safe and sustained worker performance and outcomes.



Honeywell

Client

Aug 2019 - Mar 2020

Project Duration

15+ Members

Product Team Size (Designer: Myself Only)



Outcome:

- New enhanced user experience (UX) for the product with new features and functionalities to meet the needs of current industry/users.
- Migration of the product to the Honeywell Forge platform so that it can integrate seamlessly with other Honeywell Forge products and features in the future.
- Business Impact: 6X ROI for customers in 2 years, by training only.
