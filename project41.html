Digital Connected Worker

Challenge:

Workers on the field often feel disconnected because contextualised information is not available as per their need. Uninformed maintenance (in silos) impacts productivity and causes plant downtime. Workers today expect seamless communication and collaboration backed by smart, accessible assistance on the go.

Client: Honeywell International Inc.

-

Outcome:

Highly integrated, contextual, device-agnostic solutions which enable a worker to spend minimal time for accessing the information they need to accomplish their immediate task at hand. (Product Page)

Duration: Ongoing Project

Team Size: 2 Designers & 30+ Team members (Developers, SMEs, Testers, PM, PO, TPO etc)

-
