Utthan: What's in your mind?

Interactive Wall Installation

ABOUT PROJECT


Challenge:

“What’s in your mind?” is the project for government primary schools of India. This project helps the school and the students to showcase their work on the national and international platform. The whole idea will come from the reference of “Google Expedition” where Google wants to deliver the kits to thousands of schools, initially in California, Connecticut, Illinois, New Jersey, New York, Texas, Australia, Brazil, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom for the educational purpose. But again this programme will be might not work in Indian context due to various restrictions of Indian context on a such a large scale of the country level that’s why I want to provide an alternative solution for this schools in a cost effective and simple manner which suit the Indian context and help students to connect and showcase their works on the bigger platforms in the limited resources. This project will also help to connect inside school to the outside world.
